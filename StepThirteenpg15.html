He looks back at you and laughs "Sorry, I forgot you don't have one of these" he says pointing down at his shimmery tail. He grabs your hand and you feel your face get bright red, you instantly wonder if he noticed that? Can you see people blush under water? 'Ugh this has weird written all over it' you think to yourself. But then you notice that you guys are approaching a city but fully submerged under water. You thought things like this city were just things of fairy tales and fictional movies, but maybe you thought wrong. There are families living here, little kids playing on playgrounds chasing each other, there are grownups either swimming around using their tails or people in vehicles. "Welcome to Atlantix" Matthew's eyes light up when he talks about his city. What you find most fascinating about this city, is the coexistence of sea life and people, or are they human - fish hybrids? Whatever they are this city that they live in looks way more sophisticated and cooler than New York City ever will. You are so amazed you have no words. Matthew spends a couple minutes just looking at you, mesmerized by this place he lived for his whole life. You want to ask him so many questions like "How is there electricty down here?" "What are all these buildings built from?" "Are all these people like you..? Hybrids?" "How do these buildings stand?" "How long has this place been here?" "How did it come to be?" But you are so amazed that you have no words. Matthew grabs your arm and pulls you into the city through the main gates as you guys speed in between the cars you bump into rainbow fish and apologize to them right away. You feel people stare at you because you don't have a tail and probably look like you don't belong, you kind of hate it but then you look up and notice something else that amazes you.
